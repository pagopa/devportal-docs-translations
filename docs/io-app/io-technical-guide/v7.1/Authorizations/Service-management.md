# Service management

The service APIs allow you to autonomously manage the services that can be published on the IO app. To use these APIs, you must make a formal request for activation by sending an email to [areariservata@assistenza.pagopa.it](mailto:areariservata@assistenza.pagopa.it) stating:

1. the **username** used to register on the system;
2. **the entity or entities** you operate for.

You will be granted activation if you meet the following conditions:

1. you must **be registered** on the system and have created at least one service;
2. you must **be listed as a delegate** in the membership agreement signed by one or more entities.

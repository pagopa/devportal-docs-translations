# Service status

### Statuses

Here are all the statuses a service can have, how to consult them, and how they can change.

<details>

<summary>Draft</summary>

**Draft:** this is a service that still needs to be modified or completed.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Submitted</summary>

**Submitted**: a service for which a review has been requested.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Rejected</summary>

**Rejected**: this is a service that has been evaluated by PagoPA S.p.A. and needs corrections or additions. It must therefore be modified and then submitted for a new review.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Approved</summary>

**Approved:** this is a service that has been positively evaluated by PagoPA S.p.A. Only approved services can be published on IO.&#x20;

When an approved service is modified, a new version of the service is created. The approved version remains available for publication until another version successfully passes a review and reaches the `approved` state again.

You will therefore have a maximum of two versions of the service:

- An `approved`, `published` or `unpublished` service
- A `draft`, `submitted` or `rejected` service

</details>

<details>

<summary>Published</summary>

**Published:** a service that has been approved and made visible on IO.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Unpublished</summary>

**Unpublished:** a service that has been approved but is not visible on IO.

</details>

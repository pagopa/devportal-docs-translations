# Importing services into the Private Area

### What is the Private Area

The [Private Area](https://www.pagopa.it/it/area-riservata/) is the web portal dedicated to all Entities that collaborate with PagoPA.

The new Private Area is also the back-office where you can create your IO services and manage their API keys, replacing the previous [IO Developer Portal](https://developer.io.italia.it/).

The Developer Portal is still operational, but it will be decommissioned in the coming months in favor of the back office, which is accessed via the &#x6C;**'**[Private Area](https://www.pagopa.it/it/self-care/). The migration process will be gradual, and entities will have plenty of time to switch to the new portal.

All entities will be invited via direct communication, at different times, to transition to the [Private Area](https://www.pagopa.it/it/area-riservata/). Once in the Private Area, entities can begin importing services from the old Developer Portal.

The import consists of moving the services from the old Developer Portal to the new back-office, in order to **merge them all under the reference entity** according to the new Private Area method.

Therefore, the services will not only be visible to the delegate who created them but to all users who operate for the entity.

Furthermore, the services will be permanently moved to the new back-office once the import has started: if the delegate logs back into the developer portal with the old credentials, they will no longer find their services.

### Importing services

<details>

<summary>Step 1 - Request the import</summary>

1. **Log in** to the Private Area;
2. Select the entity you want to operate for from the list shown;
3. Among the active products, find App IO and click on "**Manage**";
4. Locate the "Import services from the Developer Portal" box on the right side of the page;
5. Click the "**Import services**" button;
6. A window opens with a list of delegates who currently have at least one service related to the Entity you are working for;
7. Select the delegates whose services you want to import;
8. Start the import

</details>

<details>

<summary>Step 2 - Check the import status</summary>

Immediately after starting the import, or by returning to the same page where you requested it, you can check the progress of the service import.

Once successfully completed, you will find the imported services by clicking on "**Services**" in the left column.

</details>

# 2️⃣ Associating services with a group

By accessing the Private Area and then the IO product Back-office, an administrator can associate one or more services with a user group, thereby limiting their visibility and compartmentalizing the management of the organization's services.

Once the service is associated, you can manage the association from the individual service's page.

{% hint style="info" %}
A Service can be associated with only one Group

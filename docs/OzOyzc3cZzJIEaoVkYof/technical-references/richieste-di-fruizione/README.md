# Service requests

### What they are and what they are for

**Service requests** are the **administrative process** through which a party **subscribes** to consume an **e-service** published in the **e-service catalog** of the National Digital Data Platform (PDND). They replace the previous **bilateral framework agreements** between the producer and the consumer.

Within a service request, the **object of verification** consists of the **access requirements** (_attributes_) that the **producer** has defined as a **necessary condition** for subscription.

To ensure administrative consistency and traceability, there can be **only one active service request per e-service**.

As an act that **establishes an agreement between two parties**, the request must be **compiled and submitted** by a user with an **administrator role**.

# Signal Hub

The **Signal Hub**, referred to in **AgID Guidelines – Annex 4** as the “**Process for distributing change signals**,” allows a **producer** to send a **notification** to **PDND** whenever data in its database **changes**.

Interested **consumers** receive the notification **in real time** and can obtain the **updated data** through the producer’s **e-service**.

## Data management

The **PDND** infrastructure handles **only the signaling process**: the **application data** remain with the **producers** and are **neither processed nor stored** by the platform.

## Further information

For operational details, consult the [**dedicated guide**](https://developer.pagopa.it/pdnd-interoperabilita/guides/manuale-operativo-signal-hub).

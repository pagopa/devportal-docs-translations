# Tracing

**Tracing** supports parties in **scaling their infrastructure** and enables **PDND** to **monitor the actual traffic flow** between producers and consumers, assessing the platform’s **overall effectiveness**.

## Operation

**Parties send** to PDND a **daily report** regarding the **API calls** made or received.\
The report contains **only**:

* the **number of calls** made or received;
* the **status code** associated with each call.

## Mandatory use and internal references

**Use of the service is mandatory** for parties. For operational details, refer to the [**dedicated guide**](https://developer.pagopa.it/pdnd-interoperabilita/guides/manuale-operativo-tracing).

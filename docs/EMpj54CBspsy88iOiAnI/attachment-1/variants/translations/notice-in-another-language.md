# Notice in another language

{% hint style="info" %}
In this model, the texts of the Where to pay section are slightly different, because they are directed at citizens who do not necessarily live in Italy.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="warning" %}
If the creditor has a postal current account for collections, also the \[postal-payment-slip.md]\(../../../attachment-2/technical-specifications/payment-data/postal-payment-slip.md "mention") must be included.
{% endhint %}

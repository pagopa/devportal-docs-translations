# How to create a client

### Step 1: Start the creation flow

_E-service clients_ and _PDND API clients_ are created in a very similar way. The only difference is the menu item to select.

_E-service clients_ are intended to be associated with your purposes and to interact with the e-service APIs of producers.

_PDND API clients_, on the other hand, are intended to interact with the [APIs exposed by PDND](https://developer.pagopa.it/pdnd-interoperabilita/api).

From the front office, go to the menu item _**Client management > E-service APIs**_ or _**Client management > PDND APIs**_ and then click _**Create new**_.

### Step 2: Assign a name and a description

Each client has a name and a description to help the party identify the group of people that is part of it (for example, employees of an internal or external software house of the party).

### Step 3: Define the members of a client

You can add any number of people to the client, provided that these people have accounts to operate on behalf of the party.

You can manage accounts from the Members Area platform, and more information is available in the [dedicated section](../../getting-started/guida-alladesione.md#user-and-role-management).

The roles allowed within a client are administrator and security operator.

Security operators do not necessarily have to be part of your party; they can also be people from your technology partner, for example. The important thing is that the party administrator registers the accounts on the _Members Area_ platform and then adds the users to the client.

### Step 4: Create the client

At the bottom of the page, finalize the creation by clicking _**Create**_.

For best practices in managing your clients, see the [dedicated section](../../technical-references/client-e-materiale-crittografico/client-management.md).

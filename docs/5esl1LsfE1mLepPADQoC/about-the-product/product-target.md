# Product target

Currently, the following entities can participate: Public Administrations (PAs), Public Service Managers (GSP), Publicly controlled companies (SCP), Consolidated economic-accounting companies (SCEC), E‑procurement platforms certified by AgID and insurance companies (only to the flow related to [dormant insurance policies](https://innovazione.gov.it/notizie/articoli/la-piattaforma-digitale-nazionale-dati-apre-alle-compagnie-d-assicurazione/)).

As outlined in the AgID Guidelines — 2025 update, private enterprises will also be granted access as consumers. Currently, they can only access the Validation environment.

To access the _E-service Catalog_, it's necessary first to complete the subscription process, which includes signing and uploading the subscription agreement to the platform. For more details, please refer to the [guide to joining](../tutorials/guida-alladesione.md).

If an organization cannot meet the terms of the membership agreement, it is possible to contact support to evaluate the situation during the adhesion process, via the _Assistance_ link located at the top right during the process.
